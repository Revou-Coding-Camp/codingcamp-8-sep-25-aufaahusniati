Our Company Profile

Founded in 2024 in Bandung, Matchacih was born from a simple passion: to share the authentic, mindful experience of Japanese matcha. We partner with traditional tea farms in Uji, Kyoto, to bring you the finest, stone-ground matcha powder. Our process honors centuries of tradition to ensure every sip delivers a smooth, rich flavor and a moment of pure tranquility.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and beloved matcha brand in Indonesia, inspiring a lifestyle of mindfulness and well-being through every cup.

Our Mission

To provide ceremonial-grade matcha of the highest quality, educate our customers on its benefits, and build a community that celebrates the art of mindful living.
